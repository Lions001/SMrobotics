About Us

Welcome to the SM FTC Robotics JV team! We are a group of passionate students dedicated to the principles of STEM through the exciting world of robotics. Our team is committed to learning, building, and competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge. Join us on our journey to innovate and inspire!

Our goal is to win the INSPIRE award for the second year in a row and advance to Worlds!
