Spark-A-Story

Helping kids share their stories with the world

About Us

Spark-A-Story is a nonprofit dedicated to empowering young writers. We provide workshops, mentorship, and resources to help kids bring their ideas to life and share them with the world.

Programs

We host writing workshops at local libraries, run online events, and create opportunities for kids to publish and showcase their work.

Get Involved

Want to help? You can volunteer as a mentor, donate to support our workshops, or spread the word about Spark-A-Story.
